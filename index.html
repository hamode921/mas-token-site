1. The Vision

MAS Token is a "Community First" project, designed as a fully decentralized digital asset on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). Our primary goal is to break the barriers of monopoly in digital markets and provide a safe, transparent investment environment based on the power of code and programmatic integrity.

2. Security & Transparency

We believe that trust is earned through facts and figures. The following measures ensure project sustainability:

  • Deflationary Model: 1.60 million tokens have been permanently burnt (sent to the black hole), reducing supply and increasing value over time.
  • Liquidity Locked: 100% of liquidity is locked (7,000,000 MAS) to ensure a Rug-Proof environment.
  • Renounced Ownership: The contract belongs to the community; no one, including the developer, can modify the rules.
