1. The Vision

MAS Token is a "Community First" project, designed as a fully decentralized digital asset on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). The primary goal of the project is to break the barriers of monopoly in digital markets and provide a secure and transparent investment environment based on the power of the code and programmatic integrity.

2. Security & Transparency

We believe that trust is not given but earned through numbers and facts. The following measures have been taken to ensure project sustainability:

  • Deflationary Model: 1.65 million tokens have been permanently burnt by sending them to the "black hole" address, reducing supply and increasing the value of the currency over time.
  • Liquidity Locked: 100% of the liquidity is locked to ensure no manipulation (Rug-Proof), providing absolute security for traders.
  • Renounced Ownership: The contract now belongs to the community; no one, including the developer, can modify the rules or control the smart contract.
